They noisily broke the shells with their teeth and sucked out the white flesh. Salty water, stinging painfully, ran down to their wrist. Borch served up some more beer, scraping the bottom of the small cask, while the Zerricanians amused themselves by watching the goings on around them. They laughed unpleasantly at a soothsayer on the next table over and the witcher was convinced that they were looking for a fight. The girls giggled, Tea blowing him a kiss and giving him an ostentatious wink. Her tattoos made the gesture slightly macabre.
